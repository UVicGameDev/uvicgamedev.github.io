Rock N Roll Soul Customer

Who do you want to make games for?

Gamers looking for a casual adventure that challenges their platforming abilities.

Gamers who miss solid platforming physics with things to collect and a creative universe.

What can you tell us about your ideal customer?

Age: 6-20 year olds

Gender: Any

Spending patterns: Looking for cheap experience with lots of content and escapism.

Language: Any. (Speaking English is probably not necessary to play.)
