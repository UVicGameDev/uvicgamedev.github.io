Market Focus

Gamers who game consciously. Their love of video games plays a major part of their life.

The age range includes gamers who still depend on their parents to buy games for them. It also includes older gamers looking for a quality experience.

User Acquisition Strategy

Build a community around our games using frequent releases and open engagement with the community.

Product Information

Friday Night Bullet Arena

A fast-paced top down round based local multiplayer shooting game with bouncing bullets.

For parents who are looking for wholesome games for their kids to play together, Friday Night Bullet Arena is a fast-paced competitive arcade game for instant fun between friends and siblings. Unlike TowerFall and Starwhal, the product is simpler to understand and manipulate to make it a more accessible experience while also making it have a more general appeal.

Rock N Roll Soul

A puzzle platformer collectathon featuring a rock musician who loves to jump while rocking out.

For mid-core to core gamers who are looking for a relaxing platforming challenge or an adventure platformer, Rock N Roll Soul is a drop-in-drop-out game with satisfying feedback that rewards players for good gaming and frees them from online competitive gaming. Unlike most games on Kongregate, our product offers a high quality experience with tight controls that feels like a complete console game.

Company Business Model

Build the aforementioned games and distribute them through online game portals or game marketplaces to the aforementioned audiences. Maintain customer relationships by interacting with them in an honest and personal way. Key activities include producing games using our limited development funds and depending on our key skills. Partners include portal owners, console ownders, middleware developers, and contractors. Costs go into self-sustenance (incl. coffee), travel funds (for showing games at conventions), and web hosting costs.

Corporate Fly Wheel

Key activities are focused on creating more content. Either by creating new games, creating new content for games, or building up hype using other forms of media and community interaction.

Financial Highlights

Depend on consistent customers interested in buying almost every game we release once they become attached to the community. Money goes towards paying the bills and hosting/license costs so we can keep making games.
