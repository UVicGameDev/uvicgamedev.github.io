Why?

Why do you want to go into business?

To develop our passion and make a living from it. To express ourselves artistically. To live a meaningful life making meaningful products. (Meaningful for us, and for our audience too.)

What do you hope to achieve?

Creating memorable and fun gaming experiences.

Do you want to build a company and sell?

No, the company is a means to an end for us to create products.

Do you want to get rich?

Would be nice not to have to worry about money, but it is not an ultimate goal.

Do you want to do what you love?

Yes. We are inspired to do this because of our love for the craft.

Do you want a big company or a small company?

A small company. If the company is done right, you will not be sure there is a company at all.
