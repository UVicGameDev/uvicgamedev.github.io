Friday Night Bullet Arena Positioning Statement

Positioning Statement

For parents who are looking for wholesome games for their kids to play together, Friday Night Bullet Arena is a fast-paced competitive arcade game for instant fun between friends and siblings. Unlike TowerFall and Starwhal, the product is simpler to understand and manipulate to make it a more accessible experience while also making it have a more general appeal.
