Rock N Roll Soul Positioning Statement

Positioning Statement

For mid-core to core gamers who are looking for a relaxing platforming challenge or an adventure platformer, Rock N Roll Soul is a drop-in-drop-out game with satisfying feedback that rewards players for good gaming and frees them from online competitive gaming. Unlike most games on Kongregate, our product offers a high quality experience with tight controls that feels like a complete console game.

Channel Approach

Could be offered on the web on portals like Kongregate and Armor Games. Could also sell the game through a service like Steam. If we can pull strings, we can get it on consoles.

User Acquisition Strategy

  • Charm audiences with graphics and satisfying feedback.
  • Get people excited about going on an adventure in a beautiful world.
  • Get game on youtube lets play channels to get young (and old) audiences.
  • Release game after building up hype.
