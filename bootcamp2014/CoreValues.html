Core Values

What is a core value? (According to slides)

  • A belief which is discovered, not created.
  • Assumption which you believe is correct.
  • A belief you will follow, even if it is not in your best interest.

FruitPoncho Core Values

  • Authenticity: Stand for your beliefs, do what you love, be honest with others.
  • Craftsmanship: Put your best foot forward on every task. Devote your life to perfecting your craft.
  • Create Memories: Go the extra mile to make the experience unique.
